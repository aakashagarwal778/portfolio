Brent Crude Under OPEC+ Band Enforcement: A Jump-Diffusion SDE Approach

Geoeconomic SDE Series · Case 01 LaTeX PDF · Calibration data Data: BZ=F · 4,648 obs · 2000–2026 April 2025
Download PDF ↗ View Python code ↗ Open live model →
Snapshot

Brent crude does not random-walk indefinitely. Over medium horizons it exhibits persistent mean-reversion toward a structural band of approximately $70–$90, enforced by OPEC+ production decisions and US shale supply response. This document derives a jump-diffusion SDE from those economic mechanisms, calibrates it via MLE on 4,648 daily observations, and produces tradeable probability distributions over price horizons.

Key result — 30-day horizon from $82
P(in $70–$90 band): 71.7%
P(>$90): 19.1% · P(<$70): 9.2% · Asymmetry from OU drift toward θ = $87.11

\(dX = \kappa(\theta-X)\,dt + \sigma_0(1+\alpha|X-\theta|)\,dW + J\,dq\)
