About

MSc Quantitative Finance at Kiel University, with industry experience at Solactive AG in Fixed Income QIS. There I co-developed a BDC index that launched as a NASDAQ-listed ETF and built calibration frameworks across institutional fixed income products.

My technical focus is stochastic processes and derivatives pricing, with an emphasis on building models from first principles rather than adapting standard forms. The work is grounded in full derivations, explicit tractability checks before any numerical step, and calibration against real market data throughout.
